ChoreBot

Your Smart Robot for Household Chores

About ChoreBot

ChoreBot is an innovative robot designed to make your life easier by handling everyday household chores. Invented by [Your Little Brother's Name], this robot can wash dishes, mop floors, and vacuum your home efficiently.

Perfect for busy families, ChoreBot uses advanced technology to navigate your home and perform tasks with precision.

Features

  • Dish Washing: Automatically loads and cleans dishes in your dishwasher.
  • Mopping: Detects and cleans spills and dirt on floors.
  • Vacuuming: Picks up dust, crumbs, and pet hair from carpets and hard floors.
  • Phone Connectivity: Control ChoreBot from your phone via this web app. Upload custom code to program behaviors and pictures of your home for better navigation.

Control Panel (Phone-Friendly)

Use your phone to connect to ChoreBot. Upload code for custom tasks or pictures of your dishwasher, vacuum, and house layout to make operations more precise.

How It Works

1. Connect your phone to the robot via Bluetooth or Wi-Fi.

2. Use the upload form above to send custom code that tells ChoreBot what to do.

3. Upload photos of your dishwasher, vacuum, and home to help the robot recognize and navigate precisely.

ChoreBot processes the images using AI to map your space and optimize chore paths.
